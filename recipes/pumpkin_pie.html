Pumpkin Pie

Description

The one and only pumpkin pie! Make this traditional dessert the perfect ending to a Thanksgiving feast.

Ingredients
  • 1 can pumpkin puree
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 unbaked pie crust
Steps
  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F
  2. Whisk puree, condensed milk, eggs, cinnamon, ginger, nuttmget, and salt together until smooth
  3. Pour into crust
  4. Bake for 15 minutes
